About Us

Shivsagar Sweets Special !

Services by our side -

Shivsagar Sweets which is started Mr. Kanaram choudhary proud to be your trusted destination for high-quality sweets and snacks, serving the people of Dahanu City with an unparalleled blend of tradition, taste, and hospitality. Our shop offers a wide array of authentic Indian sweets, including laddoos, pedas, kaju katli, rasgullas, gulab jamuns, jalebis, and more, each crafted with care using premium ingredients to ensure unmatched freshness and flavor. Beyond sweets, we also specialize in savory snacks like samosas, kachoris, chivda, and farsan to cater to every craving.

Our services extend to bulk orders, ensuring timely delivery for large-scale functions while maintaining the highest standards of taste and quality. For health-conscious customers, we proudly offer a range of sugar-free and low-calorie sweets, so everyone can indulge guilt-free. We also provide seasonal delicacies such as modaks, gujiyas, and ghee-rich traditional treats, which reflect the essence of Indian festivals and cultural heritage.
